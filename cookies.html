Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how Frosty Garden Pruning uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, deliver personalized content, and comply with legal obligations.

Introduction

Cookies are tiny text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They allow websites to recognize you, remember preferences, and collect analytics data.

At Frosty Garden Pruning, we use cookies to ensure a smooth, personalized user experience and to understand how our site is used.

Below is detailed information about the types of cookies we use, how we use them, your rights, and how to manage your preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that store information about your visit and preferences. They provide benefits such as:

  • Keeping you logged in during sessions
  • Remembering your language or region preferences
  • Tracking visitor behavior for site improvements
  • Delivering relevant marketing and advertisements tailored to your interests

Cookies are safe and cannot carry viruses or malware.

Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for website operation and security.

Examples include session management and authentication cookies.

Performance Cookies

Performance cookies collect anonymous usage data to help optimize site performance.

They tell us which pages are most visited and if there are issues.

Functionality Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences for a more tailored browsing experience.

Targeting and Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant ads and measure advertising effectiveness.

Third-Party Cookies

We use trusted third-party services such as Google Analytics, advertising networks, and social media platforms. Their use of cookies is subject to their privacy policies.

How We Use Cookies

  • To provide essential website functionality and navigation stability
  • To personalize content and user experience
  • To analyze site usage and trends
  • To enhance security and prevent fraud
  • To deliver customized marketing and advertising

Your Cookie Choices

Upon your first visit, our cookie consent dialog will appear allowing you to either accept or decline non-essential cookies.

You can change your preferences at any time via your browser settings or by contacting us.

Managing Cookies in Browsers

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may reduce website functionality.

  • Google Chrome Cookie Settings
  • Mozilla Firefox Cookie Settings
  • Apple Safari Cookies
  • Microsoft Edge Cookies

Legal Compliance and Regulations

We comply with global privacy laws like GDPR and CCPA that regulate cookie usage and require transparency and user consent.

Duration of Cookies

Cookies may remain on your device for a session or persist for years depending on their purpose.

Do Not Track

Our website currently does not respond to Do Not Track signals due to lack of accepted standards in the industry.

This Cookies Policy may change periodically. We recommend checking this page regularly for updates.
